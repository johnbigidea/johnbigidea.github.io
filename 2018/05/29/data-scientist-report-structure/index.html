data scientist report structure

Posted on 2018-05-29

as a data scientist, you are expected to do a thorougn analysis with appropriate tools. as a writer you are responsible for communicating your finds to the readers. Transport policy, a leading research publicaion in transportation planning, offers a checklist fo authors interested in publishing with the journal. the checklist is a series of questions autors are expected to consider before submitting their manuscript to the jounral. I believe the checklist is useful for budding date scientist and, therefore, ihave reproduced it verbatim for their benefit.

have you told readers, at the outset, what they might gain by reading your paper?

have you made the aim of your work clear?

have you explained the significance of yuo contribution?

have you set your work in the appropriate context by giving sufficient background(including a complete set of relevant referenes) to your work?

have you addressed the question of practically and usefulness?

have you identified future developments that might results from your work?

have you structured you paper in a clear and logical fashion?
